About us

We're All For You.

Zenzele Fitness Group is a 100% South African, black owned and operated gym management and operations company. We provide full turnkey wellness solutions to companies, educational institutions, government establishments, and to our very own commercial facilities.

We work with leading partners in the health and wellness industry to offer the best in class solutions to our clients, in turn ensuring the ongoing positive impact on the productivity and performance of all who join.

Who we are
Corporate

Improve the health, productivity and performance of your employees.

Educational Institutes

Keep student’s mind sharp, improve their concentration and keep their bodies healthier while they live their best student life.

Government Establishments

Boost public servant’s performance and productivity at work with our cutting-edge wellness solutions and better serve the nation.

Commercial Facilities

Accessible fitness facilities at affordable rates for everyone; young or old for a better you.
